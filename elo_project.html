Model notes

Methodology & Calibration

Elo ratings

Ratings update after each recorded UFC fight. The expected score uses the standard Elo logistic curve, while rating movement accounts for experience, inactivity decay, and result type in the generated advanced dataset.

Matchup projections

The Matchup Lab defaults to the final scenario-based ensemble and lets each component score be inspected independently. Two directional outputs are reconciled into complementary win probabilities.

Fair implied odds

Fair American and decimal odds are calculated directly from each projected probability with no sportsbook margin. The displayed 95% ranges visualize model uncertainty and are not guarantees.

Fight-card model

Generated card probabilities combine base logistic, style matchup, multi-fight performance windows, gradient boosting, and neural-network scores through experience-specific scenario models. Opposite-direction outputs are reconciled into complementary matchup probabilities.

Insufficient data

Fights marked nef by the source pipeline are not assigned a probability. They remain visible in a separate section instead of being mixed into the ranked predictions.
