Model notes

Methodology & Calibration

Elo ratings

Ratings update after each recorded UFC fight. The expected score uses the standard Elo logistic curve, while rating movement accounts for experience, inactivity decay, and result type in the generated advanced dataset.

Matchup projections

The Matchup Lab converts the current Elo difference into complementary win probabilities. Its interval uses the smaller fighter sample as a conservative uncertainty input.

Fight-card model

Generated card probabilities combine base logistic, style matchup, moving form windows, gradient boosting, and neural-network scores through experience-specific scenario models. Opposite-direction outputs are reconciled into complementary matchup probabilities.

Insufficient data

Fights marked nef by the source pipeline are not assigned a probability. They remain visible in a separate section instead of being mixed into the ranked predictions.
